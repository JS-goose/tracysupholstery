Marine Upholstery

Marine upholstery is my "bread and butter" if you will. I have been working with it for many years I enjoy it greatly. I do canopies, seats, covers, steering wheels, and any other part of the boat that can be upholstered.

I use the highest quality products (top notch material, botls, screws, etc.) to ensure the work I do is not only beautiful but also functional. It will last through the weather, the wear and tear, and all the fishing trips you can muster!

ATV/UTV Upholstery

Let's face it, ATVs and UTVs are meant to be used. They're meant to be played with and aside from normal wear and tear all kinds of things happen to the seats on them. They sit out in the sun, they crack, they get uncomfortable, and need replacing.

That's where I come in. Let me reupholster your ATV/UTV seat so you can get out and use it like it's supposed to be used!

Motorcycle/Misc Seats

Everyone's favorite weekend toy isn't very useful if the seat is damaged. Wether it's gotten damaged or just worn out from use if you can't sit on it. These seats can often be tricky to reupholster because of their curves and sleek lines. It really takes a master of the craft to ensure the job is completed the right way.

I am that master of the craft. I've completed hundreds of seats over the years and I know I can add you to the list of happy customers!
